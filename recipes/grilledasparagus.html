Grilled Asparagus

Mouth watering & easy to make grilled asparagus

Grilled asparagus is a delectable and simple dish that highlights the natural flavors of this vibrant green vegetable. To prepare grilled asparagus, fresh asparagus spears are lightly coated in olive oil, seasoned with salt and pepper, and then placed directly on a hot grill. The heat of the grill quickly cooks the asparagus, imparting a delightful smoky flavor while maintaining its crisp texture. Grilled asparagus is a versatile side dish that pairs well with various main courses, adding a touch of freshness and a hint of charred goodness to the plate. It is a quick and healthy option for any occasion, providing a delightful combination of earthy and grilled flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3 to 5 minutes.
